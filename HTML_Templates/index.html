FONDART BAKEHOUSE  diaries

FondArt Bakehouse, fondly known as FAB, patisserie and boulangerie opened its doors to people of Lucknow in April 2021. FAB is the brainchild of founders Nidhi Verma and Raag Verma, who have been associated with the luxury hospitality industry for nearly two decades.

The name of this patisserie in Lucknow, is inspired from its signature offering - fondant cakes. These specialised playdough-like sugar paste can be rolled and draped over a sculpted cake to give it a designer shape and texture. These cakes can be refrigerated and can remain fresh for days, which is what makes them unique...

Find us HERE ALSO

Fondart Bakehouse is spreading sweetness to more places. We are on an expanding our services to newer locations. The FAB kiosk at the Lucknow Golf course being one of our first expansion steps. Fondart Bakehouse started with the idea is to offer Quality, Consistency and Variety of baking products to city of Lucknow. Seeing the demand and ever increasing popularity of our bakes and products, we intend to be available and serve the entire city with the goodness and freshness of our products. We will soon be seen in more locations.

The Fondart Bakehouse kiosk at Golf course is a mini set up which will be controlled from the flagship store and will continue to be macro managed form our main kitchen. While we are looking at more such outlets within Lucknow, we are looking at expanding beyond Lucknow in the near future.
