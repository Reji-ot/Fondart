The Fab Story

FondArt Bakehouse, fondly known as FAB, patisserie and boulangerie opened its doors to people of Lucknow in April 2021. FAB is the brainchild of founders Nidhi Verma and Raag Verma, who have been associated with the luxury hospitality industry for nearly two decades.

The name of this patisserie in Lucknow, is inspired from its signature offering - fondant cakes. These specialised playdough-like sugar paste can be rolled and draped over a sculpted cake to give it a designer shape and texture. These cakes can be refrigerated and can remain fresh for days, which is what makes them unique.

It specialises in custom cakes, baked in Lucknow, for all occasions along with sinful delights comprising an array of freshly baked bread varieties, pastries, cupcakes, truffles, croissants, macroons, eclairs and donuts. It will also offer a several options for sandwiches, quiches and its signature croissants. According to co-founder Nidhi Verma, FAB is positioned as Lucknow’s first luxury and premium patisserie and boulangerie catering to the city’s up-market and mid-segment clientele.

“Our innovative, premium quality products will be one of its kind in Lucknow. The bakery will also accommodate private parties and offer cake and cupcake decorating classes to the local community,” added Nidhi about this upscale patisserie in Lucknow.

What you will find here is the most freshly baked cakes, pastries, variety of breads, savouries, customised cakes for occasions and celebrations.

They are also on online home delivery platforms.

Our Signature Style

Chef Avijit Ghosh, is the consulting chef at Fontart Bakehouse, the best boulangerie and patisserie in Lucknow Their culinary masterpieces at Fontartb Bakehouse, Lucknow will be created by Pastry Chef Avijit Ghosh, who has launched patisseries in several upscale hotels including The Oberoi and The Leela. He was also the brand ambassador for various imported chocolate brands and is on advisory board of renowned bakery schools.

Chef Ghosh, a Hotel Management graduate from IHM Bhubaneshwar, spent two years at The Oberoi School of Hotel Mangement and worked with Oberoi hotels for 18 years. Chef Ghosh joined The Leela Palaces Hotels and Resorts as Corporate Pastry Chef based out of Bangalore for almost a decade.

“My Dad, he use to cook on his off days and i got inspired by his cooking and learned a lot from him and of course my mother too was an excellent cook.Its in the blood”, says Chef Avijit Ghosh, the consulting chef at Fontartb Bakehouse, the best boulangerie and patisserie in Lucknow.

To understand chef Avijit Ghosh is a process that involves several steps. A bite of one of his India-famous Belgian Chocolate Truffle cakes is the first step you must take. Dubbed ‘brand ambassador for chocolate’ by the legendary Barry Callebaut Belgium Chocolate Company, Ghosh has a shelf’s worth of honours to display. FondArt Bakehouse, the finest patisserie & boulangerie in Lucknow, where he is a consultant chef, is thus a place worth exploring.

“The first objective when we decided to set up this bakery was to understand the target audience. Second, we wanted to ensure that the production would not be confined to one outlet but sufficient for future expansion in various verticals,” he explains as we walk around the factory.

In August 2020, Ghosh began his journey as a freelance chef with Bliss India Pvt Ltd, where his main objective was to upgrade their signature brand, Smoor.

For Ghosh, who is known for the originality and excellence of his brand, it is a pleasure to nurture talented chefs such as Rajkumar Dass, who leads the team at Fondart Bakehouse, Lucknow and was trained by Ghosh. Mentoring chefs like Dass is one way, Ghosh says, to uphold and maintain his own personal brand.
